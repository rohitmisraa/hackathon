About the Hackathon

Welcome to our annual hackathon, where innovation meets collaboration! This event brings together bright minds to tackle real-world problems with cutting-edge technology. Over 48 hours, participants will form teams, choose from diverse themes, and build solutions that make a difference. Whether you’re a tech enthusiast, a creative thinker, or a problem solver, join us to challenge yourself and showcase your skills.
